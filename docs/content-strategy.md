## 8. Content Standards

**Minimum content per service page:**
- H1: includes primary keyword + location
- 2–3 intro paragraphs (150–200 words) — what it is, who benefits, why choose WeCare
- Stats panel (4 data points)
- Benefits/services grid: 6–8 cards with descriptive body copy
- Condition or menu section with comprehensive items
- Process section (4 steps minimum)
- Insurance strip
- FAQ section: 10 questions with detailed answers (50–100 words each)
- Social proof / stats panel
- Schema: MedicalWebPage + FAQPage + BreadcrumbList
- RelatedServices component above BookingCTA

**Internal linking (every page must link to):**
- /booking (primary CTA)
- /telehealth (secondary CTA when appropriate)
- /insurance (insurance info)
- Related service pages (via RelatedServices component)
- /hiv-prep (from mens-health, womens-health where relevant)

**Outbound authority links (use sparingly):**
- CDC.gov for stats (PrEP, prevention)
- Mayo Clinic for condition definitions
- FDA.gov for medication approvals
- ADA.gov for accessibility compliance
