## 7. Content Standards

**Minimum content per service page:**
- H1: includes primary keyword + location
- 2–3 intro paragraphs (150–200 words) — what it is, who benefits, why choose WeCare
- Benefits/services grid: 6–8 cards with descriptive body copy
- "Conditions We Treat" section with comprehensive list
- Step-by-step process (4 steps minimum)
- FAQ section: 8–10 questions with detailed answers (50–100 words each)
- Insurance strip
- Social proof / stats panel
- Schema: LocalBusiness + MedicalClinic + FAQPage + BreadcrumbList

**Internal linking (every page must link to):**
- /booking (primary CTA)
- /telehealth (secondary CTA when appropriate)
- /insurance (insurance info)
- Related service pages

**Outbound authority links (use sparingly):**
- CDC.gov for stats
- Mayo Clinic for condition definitions
- FDA.gov for medication approvals
